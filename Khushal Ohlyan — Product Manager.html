About

I'm a product manager with 3+ years across product management, UX research, and digital delivery — currently open to new APM and PM opportunities.

My path into product is unusual: I trained as an architect first, spent years translating ambiguous briefs into precise, buildable things. That habit — turning user needs into structured, executable plans — is the same muscle that drives good product work.

I hold an MSc in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Birmingham, which sharpened my research instincts and gave me a vocabulary for the messy work of understanding what people actually need vs. what they say they need.

Most recently at Jaguar Land Rover, coordinating delivery across three product squads on connected-vehicle digital features. Before that, I owned the full product lifecycle at Leo.Finance and ran analytics for the UOB Campus App.
