Real Estate / Monetization

99acres: Post-Finalization Monetization

Problem: No paid offerings for buyers/tenants during the property search and finalization phase. 100% of users stopped using the platform once a property was finalized.

Solution: Integrated online furniture and appliance renting platforms directly into property listings. Users could furnish properties room-by-room or via packages. API integration allowed real-time inventory and pricing.

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • North Star Metric: Revenue generated through furnishings offerings.
  • Created a completely new monetization stream via commissions.
  • Future scope included offline partner tie-ups and customized packages based on buying behavior cohorts.
 FinTech / MVP Strategy

MobiKwik: Save Now, Pay Later (SNPL)

Problem: Users in lower income brackets needed 12-24 months to save for big-ticket purchases, often relying on high-interest loans.

Solution: Designed an MVP for "Zip SNPL" allowing users to set financial goals, define time windows, and automate SIPs using existing instruments (Xtra and Zip EMI). Capital generation was calculated dynamically based on credit limits and user preferences.

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • North Star Metric: Total investment amount through Zip SNPL.
  • Tracked CTR of SNPL pop-up, weekly goal adoption, and recurring goal retention.
  • Identified mitigations for user default risk via upfront credit behavior analysis.
 Mobility / Search Experience

Ola Maps: Interactive Search Experience

Problem: Ola needed to build a proprietary map search experience to reduce dependence on Google Maps, starting with the Ola Rides app.

Solution: Designed an AR-powered "Live View" for passengers to explore categories (e.g., pharmacies) along their route. Added a "Social Connect" layer showing places visited by friends, and a "Virtual Preview" using crowdsourced images.

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • Targeted user engagement during "dead time" (waiting for cab/riding).
  • Measured % of riders interacting with Live View and engagement time vs. total ride time.
  • Future B2B monetization via AR advertising for local businesses.
 EdTech / CRM Architecture

NxtWave: Sales Buddy 1.0 CRM

Problem: 1,000 sales associates managing 100,000 leads lacked a centralized system to track interactions, leading to lead leakage and low productivity.

Solution: Authored a PRD for a leak-proof CRM tracking the funnel from initial parent call to webinar attendance. Defined logical flags (Active, Invite, Webinar, Eligible), reason codes for failures, and automated lead re-assignment rules.

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • North Star Metric: Daily average number of calls per sales associate.
  • Tracked average lead conversion time and per-associate conversion rates.
  • Solved number duplication issues during slot booking flows.
 Mobility / User Retention

Rapido: Reducing User Switching

Problem: Users frequently switched between Rapido, Uber, and Ola based on price and ETA, lacking platform loyalty.

Solution: Prioritized 3 features: "Rapido Regular" (scheduled rides for daily commutes), "Share Live Location" (to help drivers find riders in complex areas), and "Pay Later" (to reduce friction during network issues).

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • Tracked Turnaround time to reach rider, DAU/MAU, and Churn rate.
  • Implemented automated wallet balance alerts for Rapido Regular users.
  • Designed go-to-market strategy using Agile sprints and beta testing.
 HealthTech / Transparency

Redcliffe Labs: Sample Control Tower

Problem: Once a sample was collected, patients faced a "black box" experience regarding its location, handling, and ETA for results.

Solution: Designed a Control Tower UI providing end-to-end tracking from booking to report generation. Integrated with LIMS to fetch real-time timestamps and added a "Process Overview" to educate patients on best practices.

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • Tracked CTR of "Track my tests" button and time spent in the section.
  • Reduced customer anxiety by displaying test-specific checkpoints.
  • Proposed integrating cancellation/rescheduling directly into the tracking flow.
 FoodTech / Investor Pitch

Swiggy: Swiggy Regular

Problem: Independent working professionals faced the "paradox of choice" daily, wasting time selecting restaurants and customizing orders every day.

Solution: Pitched "Swiggy Regular"—a scheduling feature allowing users to set food items, restaurants, and delivery times for 5-7 days in advance. Used upfront wallet payments to mitigate company liability.

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • North Star Metric: Revenue from Swiggy Regular.
  • Tracked weekly schedules set, average completion rate, and repeat cycles.
  • Go-to-market included beta release, heatmap tracking, and PMF surveys.
 Travel / UGC Growth

TripAdvisor: Growing User Reviews

Problem: Users found the review process boring/time-consuming (75%) and lacked tangible incentives to write reviews (50%).

Solution: Introduced "Kudos" (profile-level recognition redeemable for benefits), "Extreme Reviews Metric" (highlighting 1-star/10-star reviews), and Interactive Forms (conversational UI with property background images to induce nostalgia).

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • Measured % of questions answered before bounce.
  • Tracked adoption of Kudos and redemption rates of indirect benefits.
  • Validated solutions via surveys targeting extreme positive/negative experiences.
 B2B SaaS / Dashboard

Zluri: Employee SaaS Dashboard

Problem: Employees lacked awareness of new software tools available in the market, leading to inefficiency and delayed adoption of better tech.

Solution: Designed an Employee Dashboard featuring App Discovery (showing tools used by similar roles), App Stats (utilization metrics), and App Categorization. Used ML algorithms to suggest cost-benefit alternatives.

Key Impact & Metrics:

  • North Star Metric: Number of licenses purchased for employee dashboard.
  • Tracked employee signups, app discovery time, and churn rate.
  • Proposed admin restrictions to ensure organizational data privacy.
