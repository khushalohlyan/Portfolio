04 — PM Process

How I Delivered It

Full product ownership across discovery, definition, delivery, and measurement. Coordinating smart-contract engineers, front-end developers, and market maker partners.

DISCOVERY

Roadmap Strategy & Prioritisation

Built and maintained the product roadmap using an impact-effort framework informed by on-chain analytics, retail trader interviews, and feedback from institutional market makers. Every sprint had a clear "why now."

DEFINITION

PRDs & Engineering Specs

Authored comprehensive PRDs for the core RFQ trading flow and the cross-chain swap compatibility layer (ETH ↔ Polygon bridging). Engineering used these to scope a 4-sprint delivery cycle with zero ambiguity.

DELIVERY

UAT → Staged Rollout

Coordinated smart-contract engineers and front-end developers through spec review, UAT, and staged rollout. Caught 3 critical edge cases in UAT that would have created execution failures at the contract level.

MEASUREMENT

Dune Analytics & Monthly Reviews

Tracked product KPIs (trade volume, slippage rates, wallet retention) via Dune Analytics dashboards. Delivered monthly performance reviews to the founding team with actionable next-step recommendations.

COMPETITIVE INTEL

Bi-Weekly Competitive Briefs

Benchmarked Leo.Finance against Uniswap, 1inch, and CoW Protocol. Produced bi-weekly briefs that directly informed positioning decisions and feature prioritisation conversations with founders.
